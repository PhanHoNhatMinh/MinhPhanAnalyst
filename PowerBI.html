The survey was designed to gather responses from individuals working in the data industry in order to gain a better understanding of their current roles, analyze the most commonly used programming languages, the satisfaction rate in terms of work/life balance and salary and other factors.

The dashboard provides several key insights:

1. The survey had a total of 630 participants.

2. The average age of the data participants in the survey was 30.

3. Python was the most popular programming language among data professionals, followed by R.

4. Data scientists had the highest average salary among various data jobs, reach to $94k.

5. 42% of respondents found it neither easy nor difficult to enter the data profession.

6. Visualizations indicate that data professionals are not satisfied with their current salaries.

7. Over 50% of respondents reported being happy with their work-life balance.
