PROJECT 1: WEEKLY B2B & B2C OPERATION ANALYZING REPORT

Weekly B2B & B2C operation report is one of DIAG projects. This project aims to evaluate DIAG operations through both B2B and B2C channels. Additionally, we can assess the performance of couriers responsible for delivering samples and DIAG branches where samples were taken. Through this analysis, we can give an insight, identify root causes and implement solutions for company development.











TAT EVALUATING

Evaluating, urgent handling with branches which had productivity below average.










THE EFFICIENCY OF BRANCHES

Scrubbing data from dirty(raw) to clean. Assessing the performance of Diag branches by analyzing their turn around time and other relevant factors.











COURIER'S PERFORMANCES

Utilizing functions to consolidate data from two or more sheets. Assessing employee performance by calculating kilometers delivered per employee per week/day. Identify and implement solutions for employees with performance below average.









RAW DATA









SUMMARIZE:

Functions and expertise were employed to cleanse the raw data (consisting of up to 10,000 lines). Subsequently, it was transformed into a practical board by integrating multiple raw data sets using complex Excel functions such as Sumifs, Countifs, Vlookup, and Index. From these board, an effective dashboard was created to solve business requirements, analyze root cause and proposing CAPA (Corrective actions - Preventive actions)
